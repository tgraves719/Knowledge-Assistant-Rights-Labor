# Commercial Licensing for KARL

KARL is free software under AGPL-3.0 for unions, workers, and non-commercial use.

## When a Commercial License Is Required

A commercial license is required if KARL is used by:
- Employers
- Management firms
- HR vendors
- Legal compliance vendors
- Consulting firms
- Any organization acting on behalf of management

AND any of the following apply:
- KARL is deployed internally without source disclosure
- KARL is modified and not released under AGPL
- KARL is integrated into proprietary systems
- KARL is offered as part of a paid product or service

## What a Commercial License Provides

- Permission to use KARL without AGPL disclosure requirements
- Optional support and customization
- Legal clarity for enterprise deployment
