This instrument records the acceptance, by the contributor named below, of the KARL Individual Contributor License Agreement, and grants the Project Steward the rights that allow KARL to be offered under both its open-source and commercial licenses.

Summary of the Grant

The contributor retains copyright in their own contributions and grants the Project Steward a perpetual, worldwide, non-exclusive, royalty-free, irrevocable license to use, modify, and distribute those contributions — and, critically, the right to license them under additional terms, including proprietary or commercial terms. This relicensing grant is what enables KARL's dual-license model: free under AGPL-3.0 for unions and workers, and under a separate commercial license for management-side use.

The Contributor Affirms

  1. They are legally entitled to grant this license, and each contribution is their original creation or one they have sufficient rights to submit.
  2. Where an employer holds rights to their work, they have that employer's permission to contribute, or the employer has waived such rights.
  3. They support KARL's union-first mission and will not knowingly submit work designed to enable worker surveillance, coercion, or anti-organizing activity.
