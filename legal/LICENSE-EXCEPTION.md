# KARL License Exception & Clarification

KARL is licensed under the GNU Affero General Public License v3.0 (AGPL-3.0).

## Union-First Use

The authors of KARL explicitly intend and encourage its use by:
- Labor unions
- Worker cooperatives
- Mutual aid organizations
- Rank-and-file workers
- Worker-led advocacy groups

Such users may deploy, modify, and distribute KARL freely under the AGPL.

## Network Use Disclosure

Any organization that deploys KARL or a derivative work as a networked service
(SaaS, internal portal, or automated assistant) must provide full corresponding
source code to all users of that service, as required by AGPL-3.0.

## Anti-Extraction Notice (Non-Binding)

The authors oppose the use of KARL for:
- Union busting
- Employee surveillance beyond contract enforcement
- Predictive discipline or retaliation
- Coercive productivity scoring
- Anti-organizing activity

This notice expresses the ethical intent of the project and serves as a
norm-setting declaration for contributors and users.
