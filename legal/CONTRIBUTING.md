# Contributing to KARL

By contributing to KARL, you agree that:

- Your contributions are licensed to the public under **AGPL-3.0** (the project's outbound
  license), **and**
- You sign the **Contributor License Agreement** (`legal/CLA.md`), which grants the Project
  Steward the right to also license your contributions under additional terms — including the
  commercial license (`legal/COMMERCIAL-LICENSE.md`). You retain copyright to your own
  contributions; the CLA grant is non-exclusive.
- You support KARL’s union-first mission
- You will not introduce features that enable surveillance or coercion

## Contributor License Agreement (required)

KARL is dual-licensed: free under AGPL-3.0 for unions and workers, and under a separate
commercial license for management-side use. For that model to be legally sound, the Project
Steward must hold the right to relicense **all** contributed code — which AGPL alone does not
grant. Therefore, before a contribution can be merged, its author must accept the CLA
(`legal/CLA.md`) and be recorded in `legal/CLA-SIGNATURES.md`. This applies to every
contributor, including core team members.

## Contribution Guidelines

- No analytics or telemetry without explicit opt-in
- No employer-facing dashboards
- No features designed for management oversight

Contributions that conflict with these principles will be rejected.
