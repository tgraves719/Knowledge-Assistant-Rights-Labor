# Ethical Use Policy

KARL exists to serve workers, not management.

## Allowed Uses

- Contract information retrieval
- Rights education
- Grievance preparation
- Scheduling and pay clarification
- Collective bargaining support

## Prohibited Uses

- Monitoring individual performance
- Identifying “problem employees”
- Predicting organizing activity
- Supporting retaliation or discipline
- Union avoidance strategies

## Enforcement

This policy is enforced through:
- Licensing (AGPL-3.0)
- Community norms
- Contributor review
- Public accountability
