## 8. Successor / Assignment

The Project Steward may assign this Agreement and the rights granted under it, in whole, to a
successor entity that the Project Steward forms, joins, or designates to maintain and license the
Project — for example, a limited liability company, a nonprofit, or a **union-affiliated or
union-supported entity** formed to steward KARL — provided that the successor assumes the Project
Steward's obligations under this Agreement. This clause exists so that KARL's current individual
stewardship can transition to an organization **without requiring contributors to re-sign**, and
so the Project can move at the speed development requires while an appropriate home organization
is identified.

For clarity: the license You grant in §2 is non-exclusive, so You retain copyright in Your
Contributions and this Agreement does not by itself transfer ownership. Should a future steward
organization require full copyright *consolidation* (assignment) rather than the licensing rights
granted here, that would be a separate, voluntary assignment agreement offered to contributors at
that time; nothing in this Agreement obligates You to assign copyright.
