ABOUT

A steward in your pocket.

KARL is two things. An engine that answers from contract text, wage tables, MOAs, and side letters. And a platform where each union runs its own isolated instance.

KARL Stewardship maintains KARL and serves it to union representatives, stewards, and members. When KARL can't find an answer, it says so.

HOW TO GET ACCESS

Access comes
from your union.

Not from us. Reach KARL through a QR code or link your steward gives you. No public sign-up.

01
Your union licenses KARL and runs its own isolated instance.
02
Your local decides who gets access.
03
Ask your steward for your local's KARL QR code.
04
Scan it to open your contract assistant.
